Life in China

Hometown: Kaifeng,Henan province.

City(where I spent my 6 years of primary school). Northern Henan (province), north-central China. It was the provincial capital until 1954. Kaifeng is situated in the southern section of the North China Plain, to the south of the Huang He (Yellow River), in an area where a number of streams flow southeastward into the Huai River.

The food has always been my favourite when I lived there.
